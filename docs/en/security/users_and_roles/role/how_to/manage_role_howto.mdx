# Manage custom roles

## Introduction

Support updating the display name and description of custom roles on the platform.

### Procedure

1. In the left navigation bar, click **Users** > **Roles**.

2. Click the name of the ***role to be updated***.

3. Click **Actions** > **Update Basics** in the upper right corner of the page.

4. Update the **Display name** and **Description** of the role.



5. Click **Update**.

## Update custom role permission information

Support updating the permission information of custom roles on the platform, and adding or deleting the operation permissions of roles on resources (including custom resources) on the platform.

### Procedure

1. In the left navigation bar, click **Users** > **Roles**.

2. Click the name of the ***role to be updated***.

3. Click **Actions** > **Update Role Permissions** in the upper right corner of the permission area.

4. On the **Update Role Permissions** page.

5. Click **Confirm**.

## Copy an existing role into a new role

You can copy an existing role, including system roles and custom roles, on the platform to create a new role. When copying a role, all permission information of the existing role will be copied. You can add or remove permissions for the new role based on your actual usage scenario.



**Caution**: The permissions of the new role cannot exceed the permissions of the role to which the creator belongs.

### Procedure

1. In the left navigation bar, click **Users** > **Roles**.

2. Click the name of the ***role to be copied***.

3. Click **Actions** > **Copy as new role** in the upper right corner of the page.

4. On the **Copy as new role** page, you can configure the name, display name, description, type.

5. Click **Create**.

## Delete the custom role

You can delete a custom role that is no longer in use.

**Caution**: When a custom role is deleted, the binding relationship between the role and users will be deleted.



- Users who have been assigned the role will no longer have the permissions of the role.

- The role will be removed from the user's list of roles.

### Procedure

1. In the left navigation bar, click **Users** > **Roles**.

2. Click the name of the ***role to be deleted***.

3. Click **Actions** > **Delete** in the upper right corner of the page.

4. Enter the name of the role and click **Delete**.
